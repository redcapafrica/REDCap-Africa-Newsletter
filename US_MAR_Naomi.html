User Spotlight

Dr Patricia Swart
DIPLOMICS, South Africa

Dr Patricia Swart has been using REDCap since her postdoctoral days, and she hasn’t looked back. What started with mastering the fundamentals — creating projects and adding fields — has evolved into leading the implementation of a REDCap instance that now supports an entirely new user base.

Drawn to REDCap’s flexibility, sustainability, and strong global community, Dr Swart spearheaded its adoption within DIPLOMICS, a national research infrastructure programme of South Africa’s Department of Science, Technology and Innovation (DSTI), in her role as Bioinformatics Coordinator.

Today, REDCap underpins some of DIPLOMICS’ most ambitious and high-profile initiatives, including the 1KSA project — a multisite effort to sequence more than 1,000 genomes central to South Africa’s biodiversity.

Following REDCap Africa 2025, the use of REDCap within DIPLOMICS expanded even further, powering training and internship programmes alongside core operations. This growth highlights how REDCap can evolve from a practical data capture tool into critical infrastructure for collaborative science and long-term capacity building.
