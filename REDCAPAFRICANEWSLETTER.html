User Spotlight

Naomi Njenga
African Population and Health Research Center, Kenya

Naomi Njenga is a Monitoring and Evaluation Officer at the African Population and Health Research Center (APHRC), where she works within the Research Capacity Strengthening Division. In this role, she supports several initiatives across the division, including the Consortium for Advanced Research Training in Africa (CARTA), and plays a key role in tracking program progress, outputs, and impact. A central tool in her work is REDCap (Research Electronic Data Capture), which she uses to manage monitoring and evaluation processes and program data.
